WEBSITE AS PIZZA!

Everyone loves pizza. I don't think I know anyone who doesn't love pizza. What's great about pizza is that you can put WHATEVER topping you want on it. Pepperoni? Classic. Cheese? Lots! Mushrooms? Nice. Pineapple? Hey, that's up to you, buddy. Any pizza, good or bad, can be customized to your own taste.

The thing about pizza is that you have to consume it. The most perfect-looking pizza will eventually go bad after a while. You gotta eat it when it's good. And after that, it's gone. But that's fine! You can always get/make another one.
